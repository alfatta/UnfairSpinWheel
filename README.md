## üé≤ How to Play

1. Access the Unfair Spin Wheel game on our website:
   <https://unfair.spin-wheel.click/>
2. Open the side panel by clicking the "Customize" button located at the bottom of the page.
3. Adjust the weight of each item on the wheel.
4. Customize sound effects under the "Settings" tab.
5. Click to spin the wheel.
